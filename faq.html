FAQ

Common Questions

A few quick answers about joining and working with 812 Consulting.

Do I need prior consulting experience to apply?

No. 812 Consulting is built for learning and development. We care about curiosity, commitment, and how you work with others.

Does my major matter?

No single major is required. Strong project teams benefit from different academic interests, backgrounds, and ways of thinking.

How does membership work?

812 Consulting is application-based and accepts new members each semester based on available client project spots.

What do members work on?

Members work on client-facing projects that can include research, analysis, strategy, operations, marketing, and recommendations.

How can I stay updated?

Follow 812 Consulting on Instagram and LinkedIn for application updates, events, and announcements.
