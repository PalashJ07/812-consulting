Mission

Our mission is rooted in our members.

At 812 Consulting, we place students on client-facing engagements with real-world businesses while fostering a culture of mentorship and friendship. Our goal is to uplift one another and grow, both professionally and personally, as a community.

We aim to build the best environment for learning and development for the brightest, most passionate students at Indiana University. While consulting is in our name, we are not just a pre-professional club focused on consulting careers. Our mission is your growth, wherever that path may lead.

Projects

Students learn by doing the work.

812 projects give members a chance to move beyond classroom cases and work with live questions from real organizations. Teams practice research, analysis, slide communication, client updates, and final recommendations.

The best projects are not just deliverables. They become a shared learning experience where younger members receive guidance and returning members learn how to lead.
