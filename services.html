Our Services

Student teams support real organizations through research, analysis, and recommendations.

What We Do

Practical consulting support for client questions.

812 Consulting helps organizations make progress on strategic questions while giving IU students meaningful experience with client-facing work. Our teams focus on clear research, thoughtful analysis, and recommendations that are easy for a client to understand and act on.

Because our members come from different academic backgrounds, project teams bring a mix of analytical, creative, and operational perspectives to each engagement.
