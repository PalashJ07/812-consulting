Who Are We?

A student-led consulting community built around real growth.

At 812 Consulting, we place students on client-facing engagements with real-world businesses while fostering a culture of mentorship and friendship. Our goal is to uplift one another and grow, both professionally and personally, as a community.

Consulting is in our name, but our mission is broader than one career path. We are building an environment where motivated IU students can learn how to think clearly, communicate confidently, and support each other through ambitious work.

Explore 812

The best part of 812 is the people.

Members form friendships through project work, mentorship, professional development, and time spent together outside meetings. We want ambitious work to feel human, collaborative, and supportive.

From team meetings to late-night feedback sessions, the club is designed to make students better thinkers and better teammates.

Membership Information

Project Experience

Real client questions, structured team support.

Each project gives members a chance to practice the habits that make consulting valuable: defining a problem, researching the landscape, analyzing information, and turning findings into a clear story.

The experience is intentionally practical. Students learn by working with teammates, receiving feedback, and presenting recommendations that a real organization can use.
