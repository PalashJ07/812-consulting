Who Are We?

A Business Organization Built Around Growth And Excellence

812 Consulting is a student-led business organization at Indiana University's Kelley School of Business, placing students on client-facing engagements ranging from Fortune 500s to start-ups.

We're cultivating the next generation of business talent at IU, building an environment where motivated students work together to build the technical and interpersonal skills that translate directly into the workplace
