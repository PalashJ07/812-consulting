About

Built around growth, not just consulting.

At 812 Consulting, we place students on client-facing engagements with real-world businesses while fostering a culture of mentorship and friendship. Our goal is to uplift one another and grow, both professionally and personally, as a community.

Our mission is rooted in our members. We aim to build the best environment for learning and development for the brightest, most passionate students at Indiana University. While consulting is in our name, we are not just a pre-professional club focused on consulting careers. Our mission is your growth, wherever that path may lead.

Client Work Real businesses, real questions, real deliverables.
Mentorship Members learn from one another across classes and career paths.
Community A friendship-first culture that makes ambitious work feel human.

What We Do

Student teams help organizations move from question to recommendation.

01

Understand

Teams define the client problem, learn the market, and align around the decision that needs to be made.

02

Analyze

Members build practical research, interview, and analysis habits while working with project mentors.

03

Recommend

Each engagement ends with clear findings, usable recommendations, and a polished client presentation.

For Members

A place to become sharper and more generous at the same time.

812 Consulting is designed for students who are curious, committed, and eager to develop with others. Members gain project experience, communication practice, and a close network of peers who care about more than a resume line.
