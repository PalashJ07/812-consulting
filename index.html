Who Are We?

A student-led consulting community built around real growth.

At 812 Consulting, we place students on client-facing engagements with real-world businesses while fostering a culture of mentorship and friendship. Our goal is to uplift one another and grow, both professionally and personally, as a community.

While consulting is in our name, we are not just a pre-professional club focused on consulting careers. Our mission is your growth, wherever that path may lead.
