Recruitment

A process designed to learn how you think and work.

Applicants can expect the process to focus on motivation, communication, teamwork, and problem-solving potential. You do not need to arrive with consulting experience. The point of 812 is to help members develop.

Step 01

Application

Share your background, interests, and why you want to join 812.

Step 02

Conversation

Meet members and help us understand how you communicate and collaborate.

Step 03

Problem Solving

Work through a structured question so we can see how you approach ambiguity.

Step 04

Project Fit

New members are selected based on available project spots and team needs.
